Krang v1.025

Krang 1.025 is now out! 1.025 fixes a bug in the upgrade process in 1.024, and should be used immediately by anyone who upgraded a system to 1.024. 1.025 also contains a number of fixes to C, improving the error handling and reporting during execution. See the full change-log for details and download the new release.
